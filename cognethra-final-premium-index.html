Architecture

From information to cognition.

Cognethra follows a four-layer cognitive process: ingest, connect, reason, and prove.

Layer 01

Ingest

Documents, signals, actions, conversations, governance events, and system outputs enter the cognitive layer.

Layer 02

Connect

GraphVAULT maps entities, relationships, timelines, source context, and semantic relevance.

Layer 03

Reason

SOVRA and Cognethra evaluate scenarios, derive insights, compare strategies, and form structured reasoning paths.

Layer 04

Prove

ChronoSeal and Proof-of-Thought logic can timestamp, verify, and preserve important reasoning events.
