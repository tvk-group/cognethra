Architecture

From information to cognition.

Cognethra follows a four-layer cognitive process: ingest, connect, reason, and prove.

Layer 01

Ingest

Documents, signals, actions, conversations, governance events, and system outputs enter the cognitive layer.

Layer 02

Connect

GraphVAULT maps entities, relationships, timelines, source context, and semantic relevance.

Layer 03

Reason

SOVRA and Cognethra evaluate scenarios, derive insights, compare strategies, and form structured reasoning paths.

Layer 04

Prove

ChronoSeal and Proof-of-Thought logic can timestamp, verify, and preserve important reasoning events.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Cognethra

Answers about Cognethra cognitive intelligence architecture, ecosystem integration, and research access.

What is Cognethra?

Cognethra is a cognitive intelligence architecture within the SOVRA / Entelekron ecosystem. It is designed for knowledge synthesis, reasoning chains, semantic memory, Proof-of-Thought systems, and AI-assisted decision intelligence.

How does Cognethra relate to SOVRA and Entelekron?

SOVRA provides sovereign operational reasoning and agent intelligence. Entelekron provides trust infrastructure. Cognethra serves as the cognitive layer connecting knowledge, memory, reasoning proofs, and agent cognition across the TVK ecosystem.

Is Cognethra a medical or neurotechnology product?

No. Cognethra is a research-stage cognitive AI infrastructure concept. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, or a clinical neurotechnology product.

What is Proof-of-Thought?

Proof-of-Thought refers to verifiable reasoning records, governance contributions, and cognitive proofs that can support MindDAO-style decision systems and auditable intelligence workflows.

Can I install Cognethra on my phone?

Yes. The Cognethra mobile web app supports Add to Home Screen on iOS, Android install prompts on Chrome, and desktop PWA installation via supported browsers.

How do I join the Cognethra research network?

Contact team@cognethra.org if you are an AI researcher, knowledge graph team, cognitive system builder, governance designer, or SOVRA / Entelekron collaborator.
