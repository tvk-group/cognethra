Introduction to Cognethra Cognitive Architecture

How Cognethra connects reasoning, semantic memory, Proof-of-Thought, and agent cognition within the SOVRA / Entelekron ecosystem.

Cognethra is being developed as the cognitive intelligence layer of the TVK ecosystem. This article introduces its architectural layers, ecosystem relationships, and research direction for sovereign AI cognition.

Why a dedicated cognitive layer matters

Enterprise AI systems need more than chat interfaces. They need structured reasoning, durable memory, verifiable thought records, and agent-ready cognition. Cognethra is designed to provide that layer between raw information and decision-grade intelligence.

Four layers: ingest, connect, reason, prove

Cognethra organizes cognition into four operational layers. Information enters through ingest pipelines, is connected through GraphVAULT semantic memory, is reasoned over with SOVRA agents, and can be preserved through Proof-of-Thought and ChronoSeal verification flows.

Ecosystem integration

Cognethra is not a standalone product. It is designed to interoperate with SOVRA Core, Entelekron trust modules, MindDAO governance, Sentient Signals, and future human-facing cognitive interfaces across the TVK stack.

Research access and next steps

Cognethra is in research and pilot preparation. Researchers, knowledge graph teams, and ecosystem partners can contact team@cognethra.org to discuss collaboration, architecture review, and future integration paths.
